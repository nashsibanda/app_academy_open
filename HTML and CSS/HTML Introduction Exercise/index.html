Ingredients
  • 1kg pork shoulder
  • 2 cans of black beans
  • 150g shredded cheese
  • 350g salsa
  • Hot sauce to taste (I like Cholula!)

Nash's Extra Double Good Super Quick Burrito Bowls

Description

This is a super-simple, super easy meal prep for burrito bowls. Low in carbs (except for tasty fiber-filled beans) and high in flavour!

Directions
  1. Dice the pork
  2. Fill a shallow frying pan with the diced pork, and pour over water to barely cover. Add a bunch of salt. Simmer on a low heat until all water has evaporated.
  3. While the pork is cooking, prepare other ingredients. Drain the beans.
  4. After pork is fried and tender and nice (don't let it fry too long!), weigh it and divide that amount by five.
  5. In order, put a fifth of each into your five bowls: beans, pork, salsa, cheese.
  6. Store in the fridge or freezer until you need them - heat in the microwave and eat up!
